Quiz App Project:

Created a responsive online quiz utilizing HTML, CSS, Javascript and JQuery hosted on Git Pages. The quiz is titled “Pet or Food Quiz” and displays several images consisting of a variety of pets and foods. The quiz taker is asked to identify how many images are either pets or food and is told if their answer is right or wrong after answering. The final landing page lists the quiz taker’s score and provides the option to retake the quiz.
