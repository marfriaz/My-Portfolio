Quiz App Project:
HTML, CSS, Javascript and JQuery

Created a responsive online “Pet or Food Quiz?” hosted on GitHub Pages. I enjoy taking Buzzfeed quizzes and decided to create a fun quiz to practice JQuery. The quiz displays several images consisting of a variety of pets and foods. The quiz taker is asked to identify how many images are either pets or food and is told if their answer is correct after answering. The final landing page lists the quiz taker’s score and provides the option to retake the quiz.
